Our Story
A kitchen built
on obsession

Ember began as a single question: what if a restaurant cared as much about the provenance of its ingredients as a winemaker does about terroir?

Every item on our menu is sourced within 200 miles. Our farmers, fishers, and foragers are named on the menu. The kitchen changes with the seasons, not the calendar.

We seat 32 guests per service. That's intentional — we'd rather do one thing extraordinarily well than many things adequately.
